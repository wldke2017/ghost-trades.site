❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What exactly is escrow collateralization? ▼
In high-value international trade, an order cannot move forward unless there is money backing it securely in escrow. Your balance acts as the escrow collateral required to temporarily secure and freeze the order while it is verified. Once the transaction completes, your collateral is unlocked and returned instantly alongside your processing yield.
How is my capital protected during the escrow contract? ▼
Your capital is held in a secure escrow pool backed by bank-grade encryption, JWT authentication, and role-based access control. It is temporarily allocated — not spent — during the processing window. The smart contract ensures automatic release the moment settlement clears. At no point does your capital leave the secure platform environment.
How quickly do I receive my commission? ▼
Commission is credited instantly upon contract settlement. Most escrow contracts process in under 60 seconds. Your original capital plus the guaranteed percentage commission appear in your operational wallet immediately — no waiting periods, no minimum thresholds.
What is "yield bridging" and how does it work? ▼
Yield bridging is the process of providing short-term transaction liquidity to e-commerce merchants. By matching the order value with your active balance, you "bridge" the transaction — covering the gap between when a buyer places an order and when the bank clears the payment. As soon as the merchant's funds clear, your liquidity is released back to your dashboard with a premium commission.
Can I compound my earnings by reinvesting? ▼
Absolutely. Every commission earned adds to your available working capital. With a larger balance, you can claim higher-value orders with proportionally higher commissions. Many of our top agents compound their earnings daily, scaling their operational capacity exponentially over time.
What happens if there is a dispute? ▼
Our platform has a built-in dispute resolution system managed by platform administrators. If a dispute arises, an admin reviews the contract details and makes a fair determination. If resolved in your favor, you receive your collateral back plus the full commission. Your capital is never at risk during the resolution process.
